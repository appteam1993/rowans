About Rowans Deli & Café

Welcome to the cosy and charming world of Rowans Deli & Café, which is a family-owned gem, serving up deliciousness to the east end of Glasgow since 1985. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, our award winning Deli is sure to satisfy all your cravings with its tempting selection of Filled rolls, Breakfast goodies, Lunch items, Cakes and Coffee.
Now we have not just one but two locations for you—the first on Cumbernauld Rd and the second on Budhill Ave—to indulge in our mouthwatering fare. And if you're looking for something a bit heartier, then look no further than our famous Shredded Gammon—it's nothing short of legendary! Succulent, tender and bursting with flavour!
